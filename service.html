Singing Cadets

I am an active member of the Texas A&M Singing Cadets and am currently in
my second year with the organization. We are a mens choral organization
whose main purpose is to enhance the public relations of Texas A&M
University through musical presentations, as well as to develop
disciplined leaders of character. We perform over 70 concerts all over the
state of Texas, as well as go on national and international tours every year.
Within the group, I am part of the Academic Committee, striving to promote
healthy learning habits for freshmen. I also helped organize the 130th
Anniversary for the group, where we had to contact past members, as well
as set up the venue for the after party. Additionally, I was a part of the
Banquet Committee, making sure the venue was prepared and everyone went smoothly
during the annual Singing Cadets Spring Banquet.
